Why It Matters

Small actions.
Transformative results.

Research consistently shows that roughly 40% of what you do each day isn't a conscious decision — it's a habit.

The way you exercise, eat, spend your time, and talk to yourself is largely shaped by patterns you've quietly built over years. That means the fastest path to a better life isn't willpower or motivation — it's replacing one small habit at a time, and letting consistency do the heavy lifting.

📈

Habits compound like interest

A 1% improvement, repeated every day for a year, compounds to results that are 37× better. You don't need a dramatic overhaul — you need to keep showing up.

🧠

The brain rewards repetition

Every time you complete a habit, your brain reinforces that neural pathway. The more you do it, the less effort it takes — until it happens on autopilot.

🪞

Habits define who you become

Each time you follow through, you cast a vote for the kind of person you're becoming. The goal isn't perfection — it's proof. And proof adds up.

Features

Designed to keep you going

Every feature exists to make habit-building feel rewarding, not like a chore.

⚡

Earn XP Every Day

Every habit you complete earns experience points. The longer your streak, the more XP each habit is worth — up to 2× for 30-day streaks.

🔥

Build Streaks That Matter

Track your consistency per habit and globally. Streak freezes protect your progress on tough days — earned automatically when you nail a full week.

🏆

Unlock Achievements

Hit real milestones — first 7-day streak, first perfect week, reaching a new level. Each achievement is a checkpoint that proves you're building something real.

📊

See Your Whole Life

A radar chart shows your balance across fitness, health, learning, productivity, mindfulness, and finances. Spot what you're neglecting before it falls apart.

Hundreds of Templates

Browse pre-built habits across 6 categories, or create your own from scratch. Getting started takes seconds, not setup sessions.

Built to Last

Full dark mode, daily reminders, clean stats — and no subscription required to access the core experience. Your data stays on your device.
