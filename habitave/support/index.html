Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add habits?

Go to the Manage tab and tap the + button. Choose Browse Templates to pick from hundreds of pre-built habits organised by category, or Create Custom Habit to build your own from scratch.

How does XP work?

Every habit you complete earns XP. Streaks give you a multiplier — the longer your streak, the more XP you earn per completion, up to 2× for a 30-day streak. Completing all your habits in a day earns a Perfect Day bonus on top of that.

What is a streak?

A streak counts how many consecutive days you've completed a habit. Weekday-only habits skip weekends automatically. Your global streak tracks how many consecutive days you've hit at least 80% of your habits.

How do I unlock achievements?

Achievements unlock automatically as you hit milestones — first habit complete, 7-day streak, reaching a new level, and more. Check the Achievements tab to see what you've earned and what's next.

Is my data backed up?

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We recommend regularly backing up your iPhone via iCloud or iTunes to keep your habit history safe.

How do I reset my data?

Go to the Home tab → tap the gear icon → scroll to Reset All Data. This permanently deletes everything and cannot be undone.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to the Home tab → tap the gear icon → toggle off Daily reminders. You can also manage notification permissions in your iPhone's Settings app under Habitave.

What is the Category Balance chart?

The radar chart on the Home tab shows how evenly you're completing habits across all your categories. A well-rounded shape means you're building balanced habits across all areas of your life.
